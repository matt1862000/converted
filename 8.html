Any information given to us is kept in strictest confidence by the care team and is not routinely divulged without the patient’s express permission. If we have been asked not to share information we will try and explain this as sensitively as possible. If you are unhappy that confidential information is being withheld, please talk to your one of the nursing team or Unit Manager. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION IS ONLY EVER RELEASED WITHOUT THE PATIENT’S PERMISSION IF THE MULTI-DISCIPLIANRY TEAM CONSIDER THAT WITHHOLDING INFORMATION FROM YOU PLACES YOU AT RISK.

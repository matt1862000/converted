As an inpatient, you may be prescribed medications as part of your treatment. If you bring any medicines onto the ward, we will store these in a locked medicine cupboard. At prescribed times of the day, nursing staff will give you your medication from the clinic room. We serve one person at a time to give individuals some privacy, so please allow the person in front of you enough time. This section of W.E.S.T. will provide you with information about your prescribed medication.

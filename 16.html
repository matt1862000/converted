Visits from family or friends are very important. Often, they may be the only link the patient has with the outside world. Visitors can come to the DU between 8am and 8pm. Space is limited, but if you feel you need more privacy for your visit please talk to a member of the nursing staff who will help if they can. Children are welcome but remain the responsibility of the adult who brings them onto the unit. Please inform the nurse in charge if children are planning to attend the unit.

The Sheffield Citizens Advice Mental Health Unit advice service gives priority to inpatients at the Michael Carlisle and Longley Centres. If you are living in the community but are unable to get to your local advice service because of your mental health issues, we may be able to see you. To find out if we are able to help you, please phone: 0114 271 8025 or email us: smhcab@cabnet.org.uk If you are able to use your local advice service, visit www.advicesheffield.org.uk to find one near you.

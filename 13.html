The DU is not a bedded unit, but it is furnished with reclining chairs within separate male and female lounges. There are lockers available in the lounges for you to keep items safe. Within the unit there are separate bathroom facilities, a kitchen and a dining area where you will have access to refreshments and light meals as requires.   There is a small waiting room available and two further interview rooms for assessment and privacy.
